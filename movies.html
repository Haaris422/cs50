4 Movies

The following are just 4 of my favourite movies of all time.

Goodfellas

Goodfellas is easily the best film about the mob ever made. The direction, the acting, the cinematography,the....everything is just awesome. My only gripe with this movie is that Kevin Mcallister does'nt get to finish off Tommy, other than that, great movie, solid 9/10.

Snowpiercer

Snowpiercer is one of the most underrated movies I have ever seen. Its a simple concept for a Sci-fi movie but it is executed so well that it makes you become invested in the lives of these characters. BTW its better than Parasite, dont @ me.

Kung-Fu Hustle

It is one of the funniest movies of all time.Period. Kung-Fu Hustle mixes eastern action and slap stick comedy so well that most people forget that it also has a suprisingly complex protagonist who goes through a funny yet emotional character arc that leads to one the most over the top fights I have seen in any movie.

3 Idiots

I saw this movie in 2010 when it came out and ever since then i have watched several movies from several different genres & countrys....but...3 Idiots is still my favourite and probably will always be my favourite. Its a movie about how true friendship, though hard to find and maintain, stay forever. Its funny, its emotional, its motivational, its bollywood at its best.
