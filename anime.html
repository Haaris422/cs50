4 Animes

The following are just 4 of my favourite animes of all time.

Samurai Champloo

Samurai Champloo is one of the most fun shows i have ever seen. What really seperates it from most anime is its smooth, jazzy & lo-fi soundtrack that makes the show have its own distinct groovy atmosphere.oh and its better than Cowboy Bebop.

One Punch Man

One Punch Man is one the best takes on the superhero genre in all of anime. OPM's protagonist is the strongest superhero in the world....because of this he is depressed. It sounds stupid, but when you watch the show it starts to make sense.

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure

There is no way I could describe the experience of watching Jojo's in this small section, so all I would say is that its great and its everywhere and if you want to be one of cool kids, you should watch it ASAP.

Naruto

Like many people, Naruto was the first PROPER anime that I ever saw. it is the gateway that leads people into the world of anime. Its the story about Naruto, an orphan, trying his best to be recognised and appriciated by the people of his village. Objectively, its not as good as the other anime listed here, but it is probably still my favourite anime of all time because of how much it has impacted my life.
