4 Albums

The following are just 4 of my favourite albums of all time.

In The Court of The Crimson King - King Crimson

I still don't understand how or why this album works, but it just does. King Crimson's debut LP is one of the best of its genre and a must listen for anyone trying to get into prog-rock.

Kids See Ghosts (Self Title)

Kanye West & Kid Cudi team up and use their personal experiences with mental health struggles to make one the most uplifting albums of this emo-rap era. In my humble opinion, it's even better than TattleTales.

Trench - Twenty One Pilots

I never thought a ToP album would be one of my favourite albums, but its REALLY good. Its the first album I ever listened to in its entirety and eventhough its probably not as good as the other 3, it still is very special to me.

This Is Happening - LCD Soundsystem

I only recently listened to this album and its already one of my favourites. Its just so good, it's hard for me to describe why, so all I can say as justification for it being here is to ask you to listen to "Dance Yrself Clean" and you will understand why I've hyped it so much.
